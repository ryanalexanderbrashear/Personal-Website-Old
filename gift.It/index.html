Gift.It

is a mobile platform that allows user to post their needs anonymously and help to meet the needs of others. Starting out being envisioned as an application to help users during the holidays, Gift.It was later refocused to function as an application that can be used by organizations to help their members year-round.

Features of the Application

Posting Needs

Users of Gift.It are able to post their needs anonymously within the organization that they join. This allows them to ask for help from other members of the organization without having to deal with the stigma that can come from having to ask for help.

Meeting Needs

Users can commit to meeting the needs of other users within their organization, allowing them to make sure that these people can get the help they need, whenever they need it.

Organizations

Users are able to create and join organizations, which allows for existing communities and even new ones to come together and provide help to one another. Whether the users work together, are members of the same town, or have any other connection, Gift.It can be used to bring the members closer together.

Development

During the development of the Gift.It platform, I served as both the iOS team leader as well as filled a developer role as well. I was also heavily involved with the creation and upkeep of the Parse database that is utilized by the application.

As a team leader, I faced many new challenges and learned several new skills on this project. I gained a large amount of knowledge in the use of JIRA and Scrum to carry out task delegation for my team by functioning as the Scrum master.

As a developer I also gained much from working on this project. By setting up and maintaining the backend database used for the application, I gained knowledge about how to set up a Parse database, hosting it through a third party (Heroku), and how to make sure it could provide the needed functionality in order to meet the requirements for the application. Once the database was set up, this then created a new hurdle- ensuring both the Android and iOS platforms for Gift.It could connect to the database and pull information down.

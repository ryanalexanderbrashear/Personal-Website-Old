Game Instructions

* Numbers in the sequence cannot repeat.

* Change the numbers until you reach the proper sequence, helped by the colors below.

Color Guide:

* Red- the number is not present in the sequence.

* Yellow- the number is in the sequence, but not in the correct position.

* Green- the number is in the sequence and is in the correct position.
